water

the liquid that descends from the clouds as rain, forms streams, lakes, and seas, and is a major consituent of all living matter and that when pure is an odorless, tasteless, very slightly compressible liquid oxide of hydrogen H2O which appears bluish in thick layers, freezes at 0C and boils at 100C, has a maximum density at 4C and a high specific heat, is feebly ionized to hydrogen and hydroxyl ions, and is a poor conductor or electricity and a good solvent
