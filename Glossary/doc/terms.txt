shibboleth
a word or saying used by adherents of a party, sect, or belief and usually regarded by others as empty of real meaning

meaning
the thing that is conveyed especially by language and sound

language
audible, articulate, meaningful sound as produced by the action of the vocal organs

sound
a particular auditory impression

impression
an often indistinct or imprecise notion or remembrance

notion
a theory or belief held by a person or group

belief
a state or habit of mind in which trust or confidence is placed in some person or thing or notion
